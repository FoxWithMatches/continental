About Us

Continental Aviation Services LLC established in 2023 and registered with legal entity status in Hong Kong, PRC.

We offer various types of services for airlines including hotel reservation, airport transfer, crew transport and accommodation, air cargo transportation, clearing and forwarding, aircraft and cargo handling, refueling and landing-over flight permit.

The operational department of the firm is professionally trained, highly motivated and has tremendous experience in managing and running business.

We are Sales Agent for the several reliable airlines with a fleet of the freighters with 20 to 120 tons capacity.

We have customer-oriented business model, we assure the safety of their clients and cargo, we are highly professional and financially stable. The company is committed to providing services that are reasonably priced and in accordance to all agreements and regulations. Rest assured, all customers are treated fairly and with great respect by staff that is highly trained and experienced in the field.

Air Cargo
